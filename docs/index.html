About Me

I'm a Montréal-based venture investor and mentor, passionate about helping the next generation of founders and finance professionals.

I believe in practical, honest advice — and I enjoy supporting students and early-career talent as they navigate startups, venture capital, and the world of tech.

Since 2020, I've focused on pre-seed and seed investments in Canadian companies shaping the future of healthcare and deep tech.

My background spans both public and private markets, with a foundation in financial analysis and portfolio management.

My mission: "To make mentorship accessible and actionable for those starting out in tech, VC, and entrepreneurship."

  • Expertise: Portfolio management, investment strategy, startup fundraising, market analysis, mentorship, and career guidance
